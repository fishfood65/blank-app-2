## ⚡ Electricity – Pacific Gas & Electric Company (PG&E)
**Description:** Pacific Gas & Electric Company (PG&E) is a major utility company providing electricity and natural gas services to residents and businesses in Northern and Central California.

**Emergency Steps:**  
- Use a cell phone or hard-wired phone to report outages or emergencies, as cordless phones won't work without electricity.
- Keep battery-operated flashlights handy for lighting instead of candles, which can pose a fire hazard.
- Unplug or turn off all electric and heat-producing appliances to prevent damage when power is restored.
- Identify backup charging methods for phones and keep hard copies of emergency numbers.
- Sign up for power shutoff notifications to stay informed about potential outages in your area by visiting [pge.com/psps](http://www.pge.com/psps).

**Non-Emergency Tips:**  
- Secure helium-filled metallic balloons with weights to prevent them from floating into powerlines, which can cause outages and fires.  
- Visit PG&E's website to discover ways to save energy and reduce your bill during the summer months.  
- If you need help with your bill, PG&E offers various payment and savings solutions.
