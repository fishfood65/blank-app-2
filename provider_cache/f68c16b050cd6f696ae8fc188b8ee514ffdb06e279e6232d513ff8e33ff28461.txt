**Emergency Steps:**  
- Locate and turn off the indoor water shut-off valve to stop the water flow into the house.  
- Check all visible pipes and faucets for any signs of leaks or water damage.  
- Use stored bottled water for drinking and cooking if the water supply is compromised.  
- Place buckets or pans under any leaks to collect water and prevent further damage.  
- Monitor local news or the San Jose Water website for updates on the emergency and follow any advisories issued.

**Non-Emergency Tips:**  
- For billing inquiries or to understand your bill better, visit the [billing section](https://www.sjwater.com/customer-care/help-information/how-read-your-bill) on their website.
